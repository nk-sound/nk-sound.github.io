BIO

Hi! I'm Nico, a sound designer in Victoria, Canada.

I started working in digital audio during my undergrad in Sydney, Australia, while I was studying Philosophy & Religion. What began as a hobby very quickly overtook my freetime and attention.

After moving back to Canada I started work in live sound before working in post for film.

My path has led me into game audio, building projects in Unreal, Wwise, and Unity.

I am self taught and enjoy collaborating and solving technical problems.
