BIO

Hi! I'm Nico, a sound designer living and working on Vancouver Island, Canada.

I got my start in digital audio during my undergrad in Sydney, Australia, studying Philosophy & Religion. I began recording bands and translating ideas into digital production.

Back in Canada I worked as a FoH engineer before moving into film post production.

My path has led me into game audio, building projects in Unreal, Wwise, and Unity.

I am self taught and enjoy collaborating and solving technical problems.
