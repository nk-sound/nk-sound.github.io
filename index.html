BIO

"Hi! I'm Nico, a sound designer from Vanouver Island, Canada.

I started working in digital audio in 2011 in Sydney, Australia while I was studying an undergrad in Philosophy & Religion.

Though it began as a hobby it quickly overtook my attention and free time and soon enough I began working in audio in a professional capacity.

In 10 years I have accumulated a wide range of audio work experience in game design, film post, live sound, and commercial music production. I have found that these experiences have informed each other, each offering a unique perspective on telling stories with sound.

These days I spend my time designing systems in Unreal Engine, Wwise, and Unity.
